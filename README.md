# Clemens Schotte

Principal engineer, architect, and founder of [NavaTron](https://navatron.com).

I build product platforms where architecture, delivery, and hands-on engineering meet: identity, SaaS control planes, AI-enabled workflows, and pragmatic developer tooling on Azure.

Most of my day-to-day work lives in private repositories. The public projects here tend to be samples, experiments, tools, and the occasional revival of older work worth preserving.

## What I'm focused on

- Building NavaTron's shared platform foundations across Identity, Portal, Platform, and product integrations
- Designing multi-tenant SaaS systems with strong tenant boundaries and operational clarity
- Using AI and automation to improve engineering flow without adding unnecessary complexity
